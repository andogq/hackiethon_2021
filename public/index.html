Stretch

Stretch is a web app to help you stay fit and mobile while working from home. It will send notifications periodically to encourage you to do quick excercises or stretches. Completing exercises will increase the score tallied on your personal page. By entering a team code in the settings menu, you will be able to share your progress with other users. Stretch can also be configured to exclude specific exercises, and to suppress notifications at scheduled times as required.
